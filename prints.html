Our Printing Process

CAPTURING THE SHOT

◣

We travel the world to find the best and most unique shots we can. We have shoot our images in the most wild of conditions, from snowstorms to heatwaves, from floods to droughts and from the oceans reefs to the peaks of the alps. We spend our time capturing the perfect shot.

After each trip, we collect our favourite and most unique photos to get ready to edit. Editing our photos may be a minor touch up to represent the orginal landscape, or something more major such as creating and HDR image. Editing is our final refining process before printing.

◢

EDITING THE SHOT

PRINTING THE SHOT

◣

The final process, printing, is done by exporting our refined images at the highest resolution possible and printing with state of the art jet ink printers. The print can be printed onto aluminium sheet or high quality print paper. The print will then be packaged and sent yourway.
